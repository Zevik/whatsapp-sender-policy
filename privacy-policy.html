Privacy Policy - WhatsApp Sender

Introduction

WhatsApp Sender is a Google Sheets add-on designed for sending WhatsApp messages in bulk. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users.

Information We Collect

The add-on collects and processes the following information:

  • Phone numbers entered in the spreadsheet
  • Message content being sent
  • Basic usage data for service operation

Use of Information

The information is used solely for:

  • Sending WhatsApp messages to provided numbers
  • Tracking message delivery status
  • Service improvement

Data Security

We implement advanced security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Using Google Cloud's secure infrastructure
  • Data encryption in transit
  • Limited access permissions

Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties, except:

  • TextMeBot service for message delivery
  • When required by law

User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Request access to their data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of their data

Contact Information

For questions or requests regarding this privacy policy, please contact: zaviner@gmail.com

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
